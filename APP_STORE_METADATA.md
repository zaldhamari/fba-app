## Description (4000 chars max)

Siftly is the all-in-one Amazon FBA research and launch platform built for new sellers who want clarity, not confusion.

Whether you're hunting for your first winning product or preparing to send your first shipment, Siftly gives you the tools professionals use — in one clean, focused app.

**FIND WINNING PRODUCTS**
Search Amazon's marketplace in real time. Every result is scored for opportunity, competition level, and demand so you know instantly whether a niche is worth pursuing. No more spreadsheet chaos.

**VALIDATE BEFORE YOU COMMIT**
Run your numbers through the Feasibility Check before ordering a single unit. Enter your product, supplier, and shipping details to get a full GO / TEST / WAIT / NO-GO decision with a risk score, readiness assessment, and capital estimate.

**CALCULATE YOUR REAL PROFIT**
Nine calculators in one place — FBA Profit, Landed Cost, Break-even, PPC/ACoS, Freight, Duties, Reorder Point, ROI, and Unit Economics. Know your margin before you move.

**SOURCE SUPPLIERS CONFIDENTLY**
Search Alibaba and global directories directly from the app. Save suppliers, compare quotes, and attach them to your feasibility analysis in one tap.

**BUILD YOUR BRAND FAST**
Generate professional logo SVGs and product label designs with AI. Export directly to your device. No design experience needed.

**STAY ON TRACK WITH YOUR PLAN**
The 7-phase launch checklist covers every step from product discovery to your first sale. Track your progress, see milestone badges, and get AI tips tailored to your current phase and product.

**RESEARCH KEYWORDS THAT CONVERT**
Find the exact search terms your buyers use. Save the best ones and tag them to your feasibility report for a complete picture before listing.

**LAUNCH ADVISOR — YOUR GO/NO-GO DASHBOARD**
The home screen synthesises everything — your product, supplier, financials, risk, and launch readiness — into one clean decision card. Know exactly where you stand, every time you open the app.

Built for first-time Amazon FBA sellers. No fluff, no noise — just the signal you need to launch with confidence.
